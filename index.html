About IK Piso Drawing

My name is Ismael Khimhar Sumampil. I'm a Traditional Artist and a 3rd year BS Computer Science student at Basilan State College. I started taking commissions in my second year of college, and it has since become my side source of income. I take commissions from clients across the country through social media. Each artwork I create is a chance to turn someone's idea into something tangible, and that's what keeps me drawing.
